How do I export my tasks?

Go to the Home page, find the "Export" button in the Search & Filter section, and click it to download your tasks as a JSON file.

Can I use this offline?

Yes! All your data is stored locally in your browser. You can use the app offline, but you'll need internet to access it initially.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! All data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect or store any personal information on our servers.
